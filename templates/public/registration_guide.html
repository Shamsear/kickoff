Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about tournament registration

Can I register for multiple tournaments?

Yes! You can register for as many tournaments as you'd like, as long as the schedules don't conflict.

What if I need to cancel my registration?

Use our registration lookup tool to find your registration and contact tournament support for cancellation requests.

Do I need to pay to register?

Most tournaments are free to enter. Entry fees, if any, are clearly displayed on the tournament page and must be paid after registration confirmation.

When will I know my match schedule?

Tournament brackets and match schedules are typically sent 24-48 hours before the tournament start date via email.

Can I register as a team?

Yes! Team tournaments allow you to register as a complete team. The team captain handles registration and can manage the roster through our system.
