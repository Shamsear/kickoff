Quick Questions?

Everything you need to know about getting started

Is it really free forever?
Yes! Create unlimited tournaments with up to 64 players each. No hidden fees, no credit card required. We believe tournament organization should be accessible to everyone.
Do I need to download anything?
Nope! Everything works in your web browser on any device. Players can join from their phones, tablets, or computers. No apps to install or update.
How long does setup really take?
Most organizers are sharing their tournament link within 2-3 minutes. Just pick Solo or Team format, add a name and rules, then share. Brackets generate automatically when players join.
Can I run different tournament formats?
Absolutely! We support Single Elimination, Double Elimination, Round Robin, and Group Stage formats. Perfect for quick knockout tournaments or longer league-style competitions.
